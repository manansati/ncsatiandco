About the Firm

NC Sati and Co is a Chartered Accountancy firm established in October 2012 by CA Naveen Chandra Sati (FCA). Based in Haldwani, Uttarakhand, the firm provides accounting, taxation, audit, and advisory services to a wide range of clients including companies, partnership firms, NGOs, trusts, and individuals.

The firm is committed to compliance, accuracy, and timely reporting. Our team of Chartered Accountants and qualified professionals works closely with clients to ensure their financial and regulatory requirements are met efficiently.

Over the years, the firm has built a practice grounded in professional integrity and a thorough understanding of Indian tax and corporate law.
