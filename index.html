About the Firm

NC Sati and Co is a Chartered Accountancy firm established in October 2012 by CA Naveen Chandra Sati (FCA). Based in Haldwani, Uttarakhand, the firm offers a comprehensive range of accounting, taxation, audit, and advisory services tailored to the needs of a diverse clientele.

The firm is committed to compliance, accuracy, and timely reporting. Our team of Chartered Accountants and qualified professionals works closely with every client to ensure that their financial and regulatory requirements are met with diligence and care.

Over the years, NC Sati and Co has built a practice grounded in professional integrity, a thorough understanding of Indian tax and corporate law, and a genuine commitment to client success.
